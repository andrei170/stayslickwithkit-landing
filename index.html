FAQ

Common questions before applying.

How is this different from a course? +
A course is a system you self-implement. The 1:1 is the same system, applied to your specific life, with me holding the line. Most women who come to the 1:1 have already tried courses and want the personalised version that doesn't sit on a hard drive untouched.
How much time per week? +
One 60-minute session per week, plus 30-60 minutes of fieldwork between sessions. Total commitment: about 90-120 minutes a week for 12 weeks.
What if I'm not based in London? +
All sessions run on Zoom. Half my clients are outside London. UK regional, US East and West Coast, Europe.
What's the price? +
Discussed on the call once we've confirmed fit. The application is free, the call is free, the pricing conversation only happens once we both know we're a fit.
What if I've worked with a coach before and it didn't help? +
Tell me on the call what didn't work. Most coaching in this space sells motivation. The work I do is structural. Different mechanism, different outcome. I'll be honest with you about whether what I do is the right vehicle for where you're at now.
Why do I have to apply? +
Because I take a small number of women each quarter and I'd rather not waste your time or mine if we're not a fit. The application is four minutes and I read every one personally.
