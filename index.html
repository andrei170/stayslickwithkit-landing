The Work

Three pillars. Twelve weeks.

No course videos to watch on your own time. No group chat to keep up with. The whole thing is built around private sessions and the work in between them.

Pillar One · Weeks 1-3

Map Your Real Standards

By the end of week three, you can spot a misalignment in ten minutes instead of ten months.

Not the negotiated-down version you've been settling for. The actual ones. We map what you genuinely want - career-aligned partnership, conversational depth, family trajectory, sexual standards - without the pruning that happens after three Hinge dates.

Pillar Two · Weeks 4-7

Rebuild How You Show Up

The successful version of you isn't intimidating. It's exactly what the right kind of man is looking for.

We rebuild presence, voice, posture, energy. The version of you that ran a board meeting yesterday should be the version that meets him for drinks tonight. We don't soften you - we install the muscle memory that the right men respond to.

Pillar Three · Weeks 8-12

Install A Real Screen

By the end of week four of running it, you're filtering every man through a vetting filter that takes the guesswork out, fast.

The screen has questions, observations, and red-flag triggers. It works on the apps, in person, and across the first three dates. By the end of the cohort you've stopped wasting weeks on men who would have failed the screen by minute ten.

One 60-min private session per week. 30-60 min of fieldwork between sessions. WhatsApp access to Kit Mon-Fri for the full 12 weeks. Confidentiality clause across the entire engagement.

FAQ

Common questions, before applying.

How is this different from a course?

A course is content you watch on your own time. This is one-to-one with me, weekly, calibrated to your archetype, your dating history, and your specific goals. Courses go general; this goes personal.

How fast will I see a difference?

Most clients spot real misalignments inside the first two weeks of running the screen. Bigger shifts - the version of yourself you bring on dates, the type of man you stop tolerating - usually click around weeks 5-7. By week 12 the change is structural, not cosmetic.

How much time per week?

One 60-minute session per week, plus 30-60 minutes of fieldwork between sessions. Total commitment: about 90-120 minutes a week for 12 weeks.

What if I'm not based in London?

All sessions run on Zoom. Half my clients are outside London. UK regional, US East and West Coast, Europe.

What's the price?

Discussed on the call once we've confirmed fit. The application is free, the call is free, the pricing conversation only happens once we both know we're a fit.

What if I've worked with a coach before and it didn't help?

Most "didn't help" stories are about coaches who taught tactics - what to text, what to say, when to hold back. This is structural work. We change how you screen and who you become before any of the tactical stuff matters.

What if I'm just out of a long relationship?

The Reset archetype is a third of my client roster. We don't rush back to the apps. We rebuild the screen first, then re-enter on your terms. Twelve weeks is usually long enough to do both.

Why do I have to apply?

Because I take a small number of women each quarter and I'd rather not waste your time or mine if we're not a fit. The application is four minutes and I read every one personally.
